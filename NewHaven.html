Basement Apartment + Commun Areas

This cozy basement apartment offers enhanced privacy, perfect for someone seeking their own personal space. It features a private bathroom, laundry sink, bedroom, and a small living area. While the apartment includes a fridge, it does not have a stove or a dedicated kitchen. However, you will have access to a shared kitchen for your cooking needs.

The apartment shares the main entrance with the rest of the property, but a private back exit is planned to be added in the future, offering even more convenience. This is an excellent option for those who value their own space while enjoying access to shared amenities.

Price: $1400/month

Shared Commun Areas

The shared common areas are accessible to a maximum of four residents, plus the tenant of the basement apartment. These spaces are open to everyone in the house and are equipped with security cameras to ensure the safety and well-being of all occupants.

For added protection, cameras are installed both inside the common areas and outside the house, covering the front, driveway, and backyard.
